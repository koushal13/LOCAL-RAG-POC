Company Security and Data Policy

All employees must use multi-factor authentication for internal tools.
Sensitive customer data must not be shared outside approved systems.
Quarterly access reviews are mandatory for all production services.
The security team conducts incident response drills every quarter.
Any suspected data leak must be reported within 1 hour to security leadership.

Engineering Onboarding Notes

New engineers receive repository access on Day 1.
Mandatory onboarding includes security training and local development setup.
Mentors should schedule architecture walkthroughs during the first week.
